Gimme Tunes

Enter an artist or band name and have a playlist of songs generated! Use the knob to adjust the popularity of the artists in the playlist (the higher the number, the more popular), use the dropdown menu if you'd like some artists from a different country, and use the buttons on the right to further tweak your playlist settings! Every playlist is different!
